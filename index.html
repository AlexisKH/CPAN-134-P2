Welcome to the Hades II fan wiki!

This site is a fan-made wiki about Hades II for a web design project.

You play as Melinoë, immortal daughter of Hades, on her quest to defeat the Titan of Time, battling numerous angry lost souls along the way. Death is a mere inconvenience for Melinoë, who returns each time to the crossroads to make incantations in Hecate's magical cauldron and try again. Receive Boons from the Gods of Olympus and find treasures to kill Time itself.
